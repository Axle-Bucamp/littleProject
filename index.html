Introduction to AI

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the field of computer science dedicated to creating intelligent machines that work and react like humans. From simple task automation to complex decision-making, AI is revolutionizing various aspects of our lives.

Types of AI

  • Machine Learning
  • Natural Language Processing
  • Computer Vision
  • Robotics
  • Expert Systems

Intelligent Agents

Intelligent agents are autonomous entities that observe and act upon an environment to achieve specific goals. They are a crucial component of AI systems, embodying the ability to perceive, reason, and act.
