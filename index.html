AI Types

Intelligent Agents

Intelligent agents are autonomous entities that observe and act upon an environment to achieve goals. Examples include chatbots and autonomous vehicles.

Applications

Ethics

As AI becomes more prevalent, it's crucial to consider the ethical implications of its development and use, including issues of bias, privacy, and accountability.
